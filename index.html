DMI COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

DMI College of Engineering (DMICE) in Chennai, established 2001, is a Christian minority institution near Poonamallee, affiliated with Anna University, offering B.E./B.Tech, M.E., and MBA programs. Known for its focus on discipline and holistic development, the college is AICTE approved, NBA accredited, and emphasizes value-based education with good placements and facilities like hostels, though some student reviews mention infrastructure limitations. Key Information: Established: 2001 Location: Palanchoor, Chennai (near Poonamallee) Affiliation: Anna University, Chennai Approvals/Accreditation: AICTE approved, NBA accredited Management: Sisters of the Society of DMI Campus: Sprawling 55-acre campus Academics & Vision: Vision: To foster technical excellence, holistic growth, and disciplined personalities with a spiritual base. Programs: Offers UG (B.E./B.Tech) and PG (M.E., MBA) programs. Admission: Based on TNEA (UG) and TANCET (PG) scores. Facilities & Student Life: Facilities: Hostel, Library, Auditorium, Medical facilities. Activities: Sports, annual events, and various student chapters (like CSI). Placements: Has a record of good placements. Good to Know: Students often praise good teachers and the canteen, but some reviews mention small rooms and a need for better hostel infrastructure and Wi-Fi
